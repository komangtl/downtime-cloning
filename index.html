Welcome to Downtime Cafe

Downtime Cafe is the first board game cafe in Bali. We take pride providing your delicious meals using
locally-sourced produce. In addition you have access to our huge library of over 200 board games.


Want something fun to do with your friends or family? At Downtime you have access to the largest board game library in Bali with over 200 titles to choose from.


Coffee just makes your day go better. Whether you’re here to work, to play, or simply to hang out and relax, we will try our best to make it go better.


Our food is not only delicious, it’s soulfully good. Inspired by comfort foods around the world, we seek to bring those flavors here to Bali, using locally sourced produce.
